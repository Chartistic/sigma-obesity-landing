Insurance and prior authorization

If you're using insurance, the hardest part is often admin. We can support documentation and coordination depending on your situation.

Sigma is currently self-pay. This means no prior authorization delays and no waiting for coverage decisions. You pay a straightforward monthly membership.

For medication costs, many patients use manufacturer savings programs or pharmacy discount cards. Your care team can help you navigate options.

Safety-first, always.

GLP-1s are powerful medications and not for everyone. We use clear eligibility rules and escalation paths to keep you safe.

Every decision in your program is made by a licensed physician. No shortcuts, no auto-generated plans. If you have urgent symptoms, seek immediate care.
