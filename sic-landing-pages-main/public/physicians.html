FAQ

  • How does Sigma work?

    Receive thorough patient histories, compiled with AI support. Then decide on treatment options and build plans for patient care.

  • Is Sigma considered a medical device?

    Currently, Sigma is classified as a workflow support system. But if certification is required, we’re prepared to adapt.

  • How is Sigma enabled to handle audits?

    Consent logs, timestamps, and export options are all available to support your documentation.

  • What about privacy?

    Sigma is fully GDPR-compliant. No marketing trackers are ever placed on pages pertaining to health data.

  • How am I compensated for my work?

    You’ll be compensated per patient consultation you complete. You’ll have the chance to provide your preferred payment details during your onboarding process.

  • What about integrations?

    Sigma supports most lightweight integrations, with plans for phased EHR integrations in the future.
