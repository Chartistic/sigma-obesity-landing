## Our commitment
We aim to make our website and app usable by as many people as possible and to align with **WCAG 2.1 AA**.

## Measures
Semantic HTML; keyboard navigation; visible focus states; sufficient contrast; screen-reader compatibility (landmarks, labels, alt text); responsive layouts; text-resizing support; periodic reviews; potential external audit.

## Feedback & contact
If you encounter an accessibility barrier, contact **support@sigma.clinic**. We aim to respond within **14 days**.

## Assessment method
Self-assessment.  
**Known limitations:** Minor issues may remain; we prioritize fixes by user impact.
